Welcome to

NETMOS

Developed under the administration of the North Eastern Council (NEC) and the Ministry of Development of the North Eastern Region (MDoNER). Built by the NERCRMS (North East Region Community Resource Management Society) with the vision to empower the NER with a seamless and affordable connectivity via transportation channels thus harbouring a new age of business ecosystem.
NETMOS (North Eastern Transport MOnitoring System) would help realise the Indian government’s vision of a digitally unified logistics ecosystem, i.e, ULIP (Unified Logistics Interface Platform) in NER. In the coming years, NETMOS will ensure connectivity, transparency and security for all the stakeholders of the Logistics ecosystem.
NETMOS will digitise the logistics economy for all the people of NER and it will help in creating more than 1 lakh jobs in next 5 years.

Our Vision

Identifying the GAP

Increase in Delivery Market Revenue

Increase in Delivery Market Revenue and the overall market capitalisation of the logistics market in NER, and should reduce the cost of logistics by product value, thus helping India reach the value of 8% logistics cost of the product value. 

Creation of High Paying Jobs in Logistics Market

Creation of High Paying Jobs in Logistics Market, NETMOS should standardise the NER logistics industry, and should enable a new generation of skilled employee force in the industry. Skilled and trained employees in the domain of technology handling, logistics management, and operations execution will be created.

Increase in Gross Market Value of Industries and Entrepreneurs in NER to the market.

NETMOS will facilitate MSME industry and will massively increase the net worth of Industries and Entrepreneurs in NER due to logistical ease and safety. Introduction of NETMOS facilities like Courier delivery, last mile delivery and truck booking will cater to all business needs.

Will facilitate easy evacuation of Agricultural and Horticultural products of NER to the market

In many states of NER, agricultural waste amounts to nearly 50%, of which the majority is because of not getting transportation service in the proper time frame. There should be an easily accessible communication channel between people in these areas and multiple transporters, thus ensuring the safety of perishable goods and agricultural products.

Will contribute significantly in strengthening the socio-economic status of NER

NETMOS estimates to create more than 1 lac jobs across different profiles in the next 5 years. All these logistics operators combined would be earning more than INR 3,000 Crore annually by 2029, thus uplifting the socio-economic status of NER
